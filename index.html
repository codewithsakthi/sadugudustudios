3. About Us

Our Story

Every Great Adventure Begins Somewhere.

Chapter 01

Every Great Adventure Begins Somewhere

For us, it didn't begin in a million-dollar office. It began in a small home in Madurai.

Long before high-end gaming PCs, before powerful consoles, and before game engines became part of everyday conversations, there was one person who unknowingly started everything—my father.

He has always been fascinated by technology. While many people used the same phone for years, he was always excited to explore the next device. Every few months there would be a different mobile phone in our house. Watching him discover new technology made one thing clear to me from a very young age:

"Technology isn't something to fear. It's something to explore."

That curiosity became mine.

Chapter 02

Before Smartphones, There Were Stories

I grew up during the era of keypad phones. There was no app store. There were no gaming PCs. There were only tiny JAR and JAD games downloaded onto simple phones with tiny screens and physical buttons.

To many people, they were just small mobile games. To me, they were entire worlds.

One game changed everything. It was a story-driven game called Vampires Dawn. The graphics were simple. The hardware was limited. The screen was tiny. But the story... The story stayed with me.

"Great games are never defined by powerful hardware. They're remembered because of unforgettable stories."

That realization became the foundation of Sadugudu Studios.

Chapter 03

A Question That Never Left Me

As a child, I didn't understand English very well. Whenever I played story games, I could feel that something incredible was happening... But I couldn't fully experience it.

I wasn't alone. Millions of Tamil players loved games but couldn't completely understand the stories they were playing. I kept asking myself one simple question:

"Why isn't anyone making games in Tamil?"

Not just subtitles. Not translations. Real games. Games where characters speak Tamil. Games that make Tamil players feel like the world was built for them. That question stayed with me for years.

Chapter 04

The Spark That Became A Dream

As I grew older, I found something that inspired me even more. For nearly a decade, I watched Tamil gaming creators bring story-driven games to life for Tamil audiences.

They didn't simply play games. They translated emotions. They translated conversations. They translated entire worlds into a language everyone around me could understand.

Watching those experiences made me realize something powerful: People weren't just enjoying the gameplay. They were connecting with the story because they finally understood it.

"I didn't just want to play great games. I wanted to create them."
Chapter 05 & 06

Why Sadugudu Studios Exists & More Than Entertainment

Many of today's biggest games cost ₹5,000, ₹7,000, or even more. For many players, those experiences remain out of reach. We believe amazing stories shouldn't belong only to those who can afford expensive games.

Everyone deserves unforgettable adventures. Everyone deserves meaningful stories. Everyone deserves games that speak their language. That is why Sadugudu Studios exists.

"Games teach courage. Games teach teamwork. Games create friendships. Games create memories. Games inspire imagination."

Sometimes... A game can change the way someone sees life. That is the kind of experience we want to build.

Chapter 07

Our Vision

We dream of a future where players around the world launch a game and hear something they have never heard before. A beautiful story... Told in Tamil.

Not because it's different. But because it deserves to be heard. We are starting with Roblox. Then Steam. Then Epic Games. And one day, wherever gamers are.

"This journey isn't about making the biggest games. It's about creating games that people remember long after the credits roll."
